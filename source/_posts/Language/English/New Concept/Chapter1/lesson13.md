>单词

1. color / colour 颜色
   1. What color?
   2. What color is it?
2. green 绿色
   1. a green hand 新手
   2. green tea 绿茶
3. come 来
   1. come here 过来
4. upstairs 楼上
   1. come upstairs 上楼来
   2. go downstairs 下楼去
5. smart 时髦的，漂亮的
   1. beautiful / smart
6. same 相同的
   1. the same color / name / city
7. lovely 可爱的，漂亮的
   1. beautiful / smart / lovely / nice

> 课文

1. What color is your _?
2. Come and see her.
   1. Come and meet my mom.
   2. Come and do it.
   3. Come and sit down ...
3. here it is —— It is here.
   1. Here + 主语 + 动词
   2. I am here.  Here I am.
   3. He is here.  Here he is.
   4. Here we go ...
4. 漂亮 /  好看
   1. beautiful / lovely / nice

   4. 你的工作是什么？
   5. What is you job?
   6. 你的外套是什么颜色的？
